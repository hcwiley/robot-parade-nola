Robot Parade


this november, come celebrate the robot parade and ball!

The Robot Parade is a community event. We encourage everyone to dress in robot costume and parade through the Bywater with us. We will be hosting a costume making session for 4 hours immediately preceding the parade in conjunction with Draw-a-thon. The Parade is starting at The Marigny Opera House and going to The Old Iron Works
