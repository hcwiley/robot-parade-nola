Robot Parade

this december, come celebrate the robot parade and ball!

The Robot Parade is a community event. We encourage everyone to dress in robot costume and parade through the Bywater with us. We will be hosting a costume making session for 4 hours immediately preceding the parade.

The Robot Ball is a kinetic art show immediately following the parade. There is a $1 admission fee if you are not in costume or do not have a doubloon from the parade. There will be food, drink, and live music to accompany the art. We will also be crowning a King and Queen of the Robot Ball, so wear your best robotic attire if you want a shot.

Artists and makers are encouraged to make a robot to put in the parade. If you want to see what others in the community are working on, come to a Wednesday night Makers of NO meet-up.

Artists who are interested in putting art up at the Ball should send an email to: robotparadenola@gmail.com
